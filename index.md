### 1. MEXT Scholarship (Japan)
* **What it covers:** Full tuition, monthly allowance (~117,000 JPY), round-trip flight.
* **Deadline:** Usually May/June (Check your local Japanese Embassy).
* **Link:** [Official MEXT Info](https://www.studyinjapan.go.jp/en/planning/scholarship/)

### 2. Global Korea Scholarship (GKS-U)
* **What it covers:** Tuition, airfare, settlement allowance, monthly stipend, medical insurance.
* **Deadline:** usually September/October (Embassy Track).
* **Link:** [Study in Korea](https://www.studyinkorea.go.kr/)

### 3. Stipendium Hungaricum (Hungary)
* **What it covers:** Tuition-free education, monthly stipend, accommodation contribution.
* **Deadline:** Usually January 15th.
* **Link:** [Official Website](https://stipendiumhungaricum.hu/)
